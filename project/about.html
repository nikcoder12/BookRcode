ABOUT US

Welcome to One More Page!

As rightly said "If you hate reading, you haven't found the right book". We believe in the power of reading and the places it can take you. One more page is an initiative which invites readers and non-readers to explore the world of books where they don't have to spend too much time finding what to read. Through our AI recommending system we recommend books to users based on their likes so the only thing you must do is to choose what your heart likes and we will give you a book you can't keep down saying "one more page".

GOAL AND MISSION

One more page believes that reading to be accessible to all. We aim to build a community of readers through this website who will preserve the art and literature by carrying stories with them. Our mission is to make more and more people with books that they will enjoy.
